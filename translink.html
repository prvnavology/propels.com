TransLink

TransLink manages public transportation in Metro Vancouver, providing buses, SkyTrain rapid transit, SeaBus, and West Coast Express commuter rail services.

Address:400-287 Nelson's Court, New Westminster, British Columbia V3L 0E7

Phone Number:1-604-953-3333

Services:

  • Bus routes covering Metro Vancouver
  • SkyTrain lines connecting Vancouver, Burnaby, Surrey, and Coquitlam
  • SeaBus ferry service across Burrard Inlet
  • West Coast Express commuter rail to the Fraser Valley

What to Expect:TransLink offers an integrated transportation network that efficiently moves residents and visitors around Metro Vancouver. Expect seamless connections between buses, SkyTrain, and SeaBus with convenient fare payment options. TransLink's commitment to sustainability and customer service ensures reliable and eco-friendly travel options across the region.
