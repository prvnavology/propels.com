Réseau de transport de la Capitale (RTC)

RTC operates public transit services in Québec City and its surrounding areas, including buses and a funicular.

Address:3800 Rue de Marly, Québec City, Québec G1X 4A5

Phone Number:1-418-627-2511

Services:

  • Bus routes covering Québec City and nearby municipalities
  • Funicular service between Lower Town and Upper Town
  • Specialized services for seniors and persons with reduced mobility
  • Mobile app for schedules and trip planning

What to Expect:RTC provides essential transit services that connect residents and tourists with key destinations in Québec City. Expect punctual bus services, convenient routes, and modern amenities including digital displays and accessible vehicles. RTC enhances the city's accessibility and promotes sustainable urban mobility for all passengers.
