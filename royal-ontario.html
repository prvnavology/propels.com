Royal Ontario Museum

The Royal Ontario Museum (ROM) is Canada's largest museum of world cultures and natural history, featuring extensive collections and engaging exhibitions

Address::100 Queens Park, Toronto, Ontario M5S 2C6

Phone Number:1-416-586-8000

Services:

  • Exhibitions on art, history, and natural sciences
  • Specialized galleries and collections
  • Educational programs and workshops
  • Museum shop and dining options

What to Expect : : Explore diverse cultural and scientific artifacts from around the world at the ROM. The museum offers immersive exhibitions and educational programs suitable for visitors of all ages. Expect insightful displays, interactive experiences, and knowledgeable staff providing enriching insights into history, art, and natural sciences.
