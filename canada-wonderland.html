Canada's Wonderland

Canada's Wonderland is Canada's premier amusement park, offering thrilling rides, family-friendly attractions, and live entertainment for visitors of all ages.

Address:1 Canada's Wonderland Drive, Vaughan, Ontario L6A 1S6

Phone Number: 1-905-832-7000

Services:

  • Roller coasters and thrill rides
  • Water park with slides and pools
  • Live shows and entertainment
  • Dining and shopping options

What to Expect Expect an exhilarating day filled with excitement and fun at Canada's Wonderland. From heart-pounding roller coasters to relaxing water park attractions and captivating live performances, the park offers something for everyone. The venue is well-maintained with excellent facilities and friendly staff, ensuring a memorable experience for families and thrill-seekers alike.
